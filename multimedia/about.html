WHO WE ARE AND WHAT WE DO

UOKKU Games is an indie game development studio comprised of a small, but passionate, team of devs. Inspired by retro games of the 90s and similar indie games today, we hope to recreate the experience through the games we develop. We aim to make games which cater to those who enjoy retro-styled indie games, whilst, hopefully, bringing in a new audience that may find a passion for these types of games.

All our games are for free on our website which you can find here.
